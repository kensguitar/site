About Me

Hi, my name is Ken Klassen. I started playing guitar age 14. I loved blues at that time. My guitar heroes were B.B King, Eric Clapton, Carlos Santana, Jimi Hendrix, etc... but I also teach other genres such as country, rock, folk, etc...

I have been playing guitar for over 40 years and have played in country, rock, and gospel blues bands.

I have taught many people over those years. I love to pass on the talent of playing the guitar. I am presently using the Fast Track method but will customize lessons to suit the individual.

FAQ

What is a good age to start Guitar lessons?
Everyone is different, but I generally recommend starting at 8 years or older.
Do you teach adults?
Yes whether you are 8 or eighty, Guitar is very good therapy, physically and emotionally.
Is it best to start on acoustic or electric guitar?
I feel it is best to start on the acoustic as it develops stronger hands and fingers. If you are 8 years old, I would recommend three quarter size guitar. Some people prefer the electric guitar because they feel it is easier to press the strings and “sounds cool”.
Do I need to learn to read music?
I feel it is good to be able to read basic notes or tablature, so you get to know how and where they are played on the guitar. However I do have a program designed for just learning to strum and play chords.
How do I pay for lessons?
Students pay monthly at the beginning of each month by cash or cheque. I offer weekly 30 min lessons at $20.00 per session. (note that some months have 5 lessons)
